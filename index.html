About Me

I am a PhD Scholar at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, India. I specialize in integrating artificial intelligence and computational biology to address critical challenges such as multidrug resistance and cancer resistance.

I bring expertise in deep learning–driven predictive modeling (DrLungker,LungXAI,DeepEntXAI), and advanced protein modeling, spanning multitarget docking, MD simulations, and free-energy calculations.

My academic journey began in India where I gained expertise in genomics, proteomics, and computer-aided drug design at leading institutions—such as ICGEB, and Jamia Millia Islamia.
